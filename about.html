About Me

Get to know more about my background, skills, and experience

Hello, I'm Hasan Qutab

A passionate tech enthusiast who loves pushing hardware and software to their limits.

My journey into the tech world started with curiosity about how systems work. From building Hackintosh machines to flashing Android devices, I've learned that the best way to understand technology is to break it, fix it, and make it better.

I specialize in Hackintosh builds, particularly on AMD Ryzen platforms. My HP Pavilion Gaming 15 runs macOS Big Sur flawlessly with OpenCore, GPU acceleration, and all essential features working. I've mastered EFI configuration, AMD kernel patching, and troubleshooting non-Apple hardware quirks.

In the Android world, I work with Samsung, Infinix, and older Windows Phones. Whether it's recovering a bricked device, flashing custom ROMs, or rooting, I enjoy bringing old hardware back to life.

I also build clean, professional websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS, emphasizing design and usability.

When not tinkering with hardware or software, I'm studying for my IGCSE exams in Physics and Biology, balancing tech with academics.
